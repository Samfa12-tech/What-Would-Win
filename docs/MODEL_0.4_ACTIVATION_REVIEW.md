# What Would Win — model 0.4 canonical activation review

Model 0.4 uses two review paths rather than pretending that all 134 profiles need the same amount of manual interpretation.

## Complex profiles

Eleven profiles with gaze, resurrection, regeneration counters, channel immunity, auditory influence, finite restraint, environmental hazards or other non-ordinary mechanics have explicit authored overrides and review notes: Western dragon, Medusa, Phoenix, Hydra, Troll, Nemean lion, Siren, Giant spider, Charybdis, Vampire and Stone golem.

## Conservative ordinary-profile migrations

The remaining 123 profiles retain their authored physical/statistical data. Their combined v3 reach and capability flags migrate by the published deterministic rules into contact geometry, physiology, locomotion and stable structured abilities.

Those migrations may be accepted for activation only after all of these gates pass:

- every migrated record validates against the v4 schema;
- IDs and ability IDs are unique;
- the ordinary elephant-versus-wolves fixture remains inside the model 0.3 non-regression tolerance;
- final deterministic powers reconstruct from the factor ledger;
- inactive resource controls preserve deterministic and seeded result identity;
- all 16 mythology fixtures pass alongside the existing calibration suite;
- extreme logarithmic quantities remain finite and allocation-free.

`activateCanonicalModel04Data` is the single acceptance boundary. It removes migration-only markers and clears `reviewRequired` only after the draft has already passed the test gates. The 11 explicit overrides are not rewritten. Activation fails if any review marker remains.
